I offer the following services:

• Speech therapy consisting of initial assessment and individual 30 or 45 minutes speech therapy sessions • Speech therapy with ultrasound (30 or 45 minutes) without the assessment. Only for clients who already have a speech diagnosis and are followed by a speech-language therapist. • Pronunciation training of sounds in a foreign language delivered as 45 minutes individual or 60 minutes group (3 to 5 speakers) session. Additionally, a comprehensive assessment of articulation is also available.
